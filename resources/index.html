Typical Permit Requirements

  • Site plan showing well location and setbacks
  • Septic system location (must be 100+ feet from well)
  • Property lines and easements
  • Application fee ($200-500 depending on county)
  • Licensed C-57 contractor information

üîß Annual Maintenance Checklist

Keep your well system running smoothly with these maintenance tasks:

  • Monthly: Check pressure tank (should be 2 PSI below cut-in pressure)
  • Quarterly: Inspect wellhead for damage, cracks, or pest entry points
  • Annually: Test water quality (bacteria, nitrates at minimum)
  • Annually: Check pump performance (flow rate, pressure)
  • Every 3-5 years: Have a professional inspect the well system
  • As needed: Replace pressure switch, pressure gauge, or tank
