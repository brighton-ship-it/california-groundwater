Water Quality

Common issues in Riverside County:

  • Hard water: Very common throughout county
  • Nitrates: Elevated in agricultural areas
  • Arsenic: Natural occurrence in some desert areas
  • High TDS: Especially in eastern desert regions

Test all new wells and consider treatment systems as needed. Consult a well water specialist for recommendations.
