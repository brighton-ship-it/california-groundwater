Drilling Costs in Los Angeles County

Well drilling in LA County typically costs more than rural areas due to:

  • Higher permit fees ($400-$800)
  • Stricter regulations and inspections
  • Urban access challenges
  • Deeper required depths in some areas

Estimated total costs:

  • 300-foot well: $18,000 - $28,000
  • 500-foot well: $28,000 - $45,000
  • Pump installation: $3,000 - $8,000 additional

Permit Requirements

Los Angeles County requires permits for all new wells. Contact:

Los Angeles County Department of Public Health
Environmental Health Division
Well permits: (626) 430-5320
publichealth.lacounty.gov

Permit Process

  1. Submit application with site plan
  2. Pay permit fee ($400-$800)
  3. Site inspection before drilling
  4. Hire licensed C-57 contractor
  5. Final inspection after completion
  6. Submit well completion report to state

Water Quality Concerns

Common water quality issues in Los Angeles County wells:

  • Nitrates: Elevated in some agricultural and urbanized areas
  • Perchlorate: Historic contamination in some San Gabriel Valley areas
  • Chromium-6: Naturally occurring in some areas
  • Hard water: Common throughout, 15-30 gpg typical

Always test new wells before use. For professional water quality advice, consult a water treatment specialist.

Finding a Well Drilling Contractor
